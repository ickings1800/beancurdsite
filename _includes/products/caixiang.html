Cai Xiang Tofu

For Hotplate

Description:

Cai Xiang Tofu is a product catered for the convenience of restaurant grade chefs. Producing in-house beancurd is time consuming. By researching on chefs' needs, Cai Xiang Tofu aims to speed up and simplify the process so that they can focus on sauce preparation.

After cooking on a sizzling hot plate, fragrances produced by the soya beans, eggs and sauce will give one a pleasant surprise. The packaging is also sealed to ensure the product hygiene and better shelf life.

Specifications:

  • Net Weight: 200g
  • No Preservatives
  • Ingredients:
  • Non-GMO Soya Beans, Calcium Sulphate, Eggs
  • Keep Refrigerated 2℃ ~ 7℃
